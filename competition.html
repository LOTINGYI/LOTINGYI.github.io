Competition Award

These are my amazing journeys ...

Competition: APICTA Awards 2019

Award: Merit [second place]

Scope: Asia Pacific

We represented Taiwan to participant APICTA award, competing with 324 teams from 15 economies in Asia-Pacific region and win the merit (second place) for the university group with the product "Thinkord". Thinkord is an open source project that distinguishes fetching and editing mode that save a lot of time and improve the review experience when users take notes.
In this competition, let me improve the international experience, not only to "do the topic right" but also "do the right topic", and learn how to promote our product in 20 minutes.

Competition: InnoServe 2019

Award: Champion

Scope: Taiwan

On behalf of the department of MIS from National Central University, competed with students from the other information related department in Taiwan, and we obtain the first place in the information application group with our project "Thinkord".Thinkord is an open source project that distinguishes fetching and editing mode that save a lot of time and improve the review experience when users take notes.
From this project, we experience a complete software development process, how to go from idea to building this project with other teammates (I primarily responsibe front-end and some back-end).

Competition: NASA Hackathon 2019

Award: Microsoft Special Award

Scope: Regional

Team up for the NASA Hackathon. We designed "Fire Flighter" solution for the "SPOT THAT FIRE V2.0" challenge. Our work is actually a signed website where we apply azure services, including Azure web app, Cosmos DB, Blob storage, Function app. From this competition, we almost stay up for three days, focus on getting a solution in this shortest time, and we use a lot of Azure services connecting to our website. Therefore, we have the ability to designa and implement a cloud solution.
